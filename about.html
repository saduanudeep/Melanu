About Melanu Technologies

Your strategic global sourcing partner

WHO WE ARE

Connecting global manufacturers to mission-critical components

Melanu Technologies Pvt Ltd is a specialized global trader and supplier of electronic components, serving the rapidly evolving needs of the semiconductor, electric vehicle, aerospace, and robotics industries.

We act as a strategic sourcing partner — bridging OEMs, EMS providers, and R&D teams with authentic, traceable components from world-leading manufacturers across the globe.

Our supply chain expertise, combined with deep technical knowledge, enables us to deliver the right component, at the right price, exactly when our customers need it.
